<blockquote style="background-color: white; color: #333333; font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.3em; margin: 1em 20px;">
  
    <b> Instructions</b>
  
</blockquote>

<ol style="background-color: white; color: #333333; font-size: 13px; line-height: 20.799999237060547px;">
  <li>
    Heat a non stick pan over high heat, add the nuts, and dry-toast until slightly browned and fragrant, being careful that they don&#8217;t burn. Remove from the heat and cool completely.
  </li>
  <li>
    Repeat the procedure with each of the seeds and the peppercorns. Allow each of them to cool completely.<a style="color: #999999; text-decoration: none;" href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-esWAerwO274/T1Xh4PiVAvI/AAAAAAAAHbY/NMkLeeHVV6Y/s1600/6573495641_064e9545e5_o.jpg"><img id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5716723658376151794" style="border: 1px solid #cccccc; cursor: pointer; display: block; height: 300px; margin: 0px auto 10px; padding: 4px; text-align: center; width: 400px;" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-esWAerwO274/T1Xh4PiVAvI/AAAAAAAAHbY/NMkLeeHVV6Y/s400/6573495641_064e9545e5_o.jpg" alt="" border="0" /></a>
  </li>
  <li>
    Place the nuts and seeds, along with the salt into a blender and pulse until broken down but not until it becomes a paste.
  </li>
</ol>

<div style="background-color: white; color: #333333; font-size: 13px; line-height: 20.799999237060547px;">
