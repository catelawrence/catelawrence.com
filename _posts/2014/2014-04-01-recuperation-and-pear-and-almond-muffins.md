I&#8217;ve been a bit behind with blogging of late. I had day surgery on friday so it&#8217;s taking a little bit of time to recover. I had some work done on the arteries in my legs. I have a weird genetic condition which causes pain when walking (and another associated condition which will need treatment). I&#8217;ve had some rather revolting post-surgery stockings to wear and it&#8217;s not been fun in hot weather. I took them off a bit too early and ended up with _massive_ bruises on the fronts of my legs. No swimming until they heal me thinks. Off to see the surgeon tomorrow for a check up ultrasound, fingers crossed.

Mr Pablo has been ecstatic at my sedentary behaviour (besides 30 mins prescribed exercise each day) and has been sharing the bed enthusiastically.

It&#8217;s not really hot food weather, I&#8217;ve been impatiently waiting for the real autumn to arrive, it&#8217;s my favourite season!

    <ul style="margin-top: 0cm;" type="disc">
      <li>
        <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;"> 3 cups self-raising flour,
      </li>
      <li>
        <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">1/4 cup brown sugar
      </li>
      <li>
        <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
      </li>
      <li>
        <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">2 pears, diced
      </li>
      <li>
        <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">1.5 cup almond  milk
      </li>
      <li>
        <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">1 cup almonds
      </li>
      <li>
        <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">2 tablespoons olive oil
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  
  <li>
    <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;"> 
  </li>
  <li>
    <i><span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">Topping (optional)</i> <ul>
      <li style="color: #30291d; line-height: 18px;">
        <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">1/2 cup oats
      </li>
      <li style="color: #30291d; line-height: 18px;">
        <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">1/3 cup sugar
      </li>
      <li style="color: #30291d; line-height: 18px;">
        <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">1/3 cup Nuttlex (vegan margarine) 
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  
  <li style="border-bottom-color: #f2f2f2; border-bottom-style: solid; border-bottom-width: 1px; clear: both; color: #30291d; line-height: 18px; list-style: none; margin: 0px; padding: 5px 0px;">
    
      <i><span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;"> </i>
    
  </li>
  
  <li style="color: #30291d; line-height: 18px;">
    <span style="background-color: transparent; font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;"><i>Instructions</i>
  </li>
</ul>

  * Heat oven to 180c
  * Place flour, sugar, cinammon, almonds and pears in a bowl and stir lightly
  * Add milk and olive oil and mix gently until combined
  * Place in greased muffin tins

<ul style="background-color: white; border: 0px none; list-style: none; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">
  <li>
    <span style="background-color: transparent; font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;"><i>Topping:</i>
  </li>
</ul>

  1. <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">Place oats, sugar and Nuttlex in a bowl and rub with finger tips until crumbly. 
  2. <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">Top muffins with a tablespoon of topping each
  3. <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">Cook for 40 mins or until cooked through. 
  4. <span style="font-family: Times, Times New Roman, serif;">Allow to cool and store in container or enjoy one with a cup of tea.
