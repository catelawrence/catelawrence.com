There&#8217;s something exciting about starting anew in a new country. There&#8217;ll be some big shifts besides the obvious ones like language and culture, like living in an apartment. I&#8217;ve lived in a couple of ground floor flats but never an apartment in a multifloor dwelling with a lift! 
  
<br /> The place we are renting is small (55m square) by Australian standards but it&#8217;s a good start for us. It&#8217;s cheap, comes with all mod cons (even a dishwasher which I&#8217;ve never had and am looking forward to using for sterilising jars before preserving). 
  
<br /> It&#8217;s a time of continual decluttering in preparation for small space living. We are of course, selling as much as we can to fund our travels (Mr Pablo&#8217;s journey is more than ours). We&#8217;ve held two successful garage sales with at least another to follow. I&#8217;ve been selling off my art work including some of my own creations. 
  
<br /> These embroideries  are of lyrics of some of my favourite Smiths lyrics. I&#8217;ve exhibited these a couple of times at different exhibitions.
