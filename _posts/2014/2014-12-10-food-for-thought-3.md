<br /> Things are tickling along nicely in Leipzig. Last week we had our first snow! The funny thing is that I have only seen snow twice so when I went outside that morning to ride my bike to school I thought all the white stuff everywhere was salt. I&#8217;ve seen these huge vats of salt for putting on the ground ( I think?) in preparation for snow so I thought, &#8216;Oh wow, snow is coming! &#8216; It wasn&#8217;t until I got to school and the teacher said it was snow that I realised. Silly me!
