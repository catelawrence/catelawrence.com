<br /> I love these biscotti, a kind of dried biscuit that is twice baked and taste wonderful with a hot coffee. They take a little time to make but the result is well worth it.



**Almond biscotti**
  
_Ingredients_
  
<br /> 

  * 1 cup sugar
  * 2 eggs worth of egg replacer
  * Finely grated rind of one lemon
  * 2 cups plain flour, sifted
  * 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  * 150grams whole almonds and pistachios
  * 150 grams dried apricots, diced

 

<div style="margin-left: 25.5pt; mso-hyphenate: none; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
  <span lang="EN-GB" style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 11.0pt; line-height: 120%; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia;"><i> Instructions</i>


<div style="margin-left: 40.0pt; mso-list: l1 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
  <!-- [if !supportLists]-->
  
  <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: 11.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia; mso-fareast-font-family: Georgia;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">1.<span style="font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal;">    <!--[endif]-->
  
  <span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Preheat oven to 170°C. Line a large baking tray with baking paper.


<div style="margin-left: 40.0pt; mso-add-space: auto; mso-list: l1 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
  <!-- [if !supportLists]-->
  
  <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: 11.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia; mso-fareast-font-family: Georgia;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">2.<span style="font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal;">   <!--[endif]-->
  
  <span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Place sugar, egg replacer and grated lemon rind in a large bowl. Beat with electric beaters until pale and thick.


<div style="margin-left: 40.0pt; mso-add-space: auto; mso-list: l1 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
  <!-- [if !supportLists]-->
  
  <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: 11.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia; mso-fareast-font-family: Georgia;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">3.<span style="font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal;">   <!--[endif]-->
  
  <span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Fold in sifted flour, baking powder, nuts  and dried apricots, then use your hands to lightly knead dough on a floured work surface until the dough is smooth.


<div style="margin-left: 40.0pt; mso-list: l1 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
  <!-- [if !supportLists]-->
  
  <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: 11.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia; mso-fareast-font-family: Georgia;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">4.<span style="font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal;">   <!--[endif]-->
  
  <span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Divide the mixture in half. Form 2 long logs about 25cm long x 5cm wide and place on prepared baking tray, leaving space between logs. Bake for 15-20 minutes until the dough is firm to the touch and slightly golden.


<div style="margin-left: 40.0pt; mso-list: l1 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
  <!-- [if !supportLists]-->
  
  <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: 11.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia; mso-fareast-font-family: Georgia;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">5.<span style="font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal;">   <!--[endif]-->
  
  <span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Remove from the oven and set aside to cool for 10 minutes


<div style="margin-left: 40.0pt; mso-add-space: auto; mso-list: l1 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
  <!-- [if !supportLists]-->
  
  <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: 11.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia; mso-fareast-font-family: Georgia;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">6.<span style="font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal;">   <!--[endif]-->
  
  <span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Preheat the oven to 140°C.


<div style="margin-left: 40.0pt; mso-add-space: auto; mso-list: l1 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
  <!-- [if !supportLists]-->
  
  <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: 11.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia; mso-fareast-font-family: Georgia;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">7.<span style="font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal;">   <!--[endif]-->
  
  <span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Once dough is cool, use a small serrated knife to cut each log on the diagonal in 0.5cm slices.


<div style="margin-left: 40.0pt; mso-add-space: auto; mso-list: l1 level1 lfo1; text-indent: -18.0pt;">
  <!-- [if !supportLists]-->
  
  <span style="font-family: Georgia; font-size: 11.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia; mso-fareast-font-family: Georgia;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">8.<span style="font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal;">   <!--[endif]-->
  
  <span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Lay slices flat on the baking tray and return to oven for 5-10 minutes, turning once, until completely dried out. Remove from oven and allow to cool completely.

<div style="margin-left: 22.0pt;">
  <span style="font-size: 11.0pt;"><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; mso-spacerun: yes;"> <i style="mso-bidi-font-style: normal;">Store in an airtight container for 2-3 weeks. 
  
  
    Variations:<br /> </i>You can vary the fruit, spices, add cacoa powder, dip or drizzle in chocolate, grate zest of orange or lemon&#8230;the possibilities are endless!
